Unit 01 海外商務與旅行 Overseas Business & Travel
✦ Group 4 ✦ 5 Words
🔊 速度：
1
advertise /ˈædvətaɪz/ v. 廣告；宣傳
📖  to tell people about a product to get them to buy it
💬  We need to advertise our new product on social media.
我們需要在社群媒體上宣傳我們的新產品。
2
advertisement /ədˈvɜːtɪsmənt/ n. 廣告；廣告宣傳
📖  a notice or video that promotes something
💬  Did you see that funny advertisement on TV last night?
你有看到昨晚電視上那個很好笑的廣告嗎？
3
advertising /ˈædvətaɪzɪŋ/ n. 廣告業；廣告宣傳
📖  the business of promoting products or services
💬  Online advertising is much cheaper than print ads.
網路廣告比印刷廣告便宜多了。
4
advantage /ədˈvɑːntɪdʒ/ n. 優勢；好處
📖  something that puts you in a better position than others
💬  Knowing two languages is a big advantage when travelling.
會兩種語言在旅行時是一大優勢。
5
achieve /əˈtʃiːv/ v. 達成；實現
📖  to succeed in doing something through effort
💬  I want to achieve my goal of visiting ten countries before I'm thirty.
我希望在三十歲之前達成走訪十個國家的目標。
💬 Dialogue  情境對話
Our team decided to advertise the new service on social media.
我們團隊決定在社群媒體上宣傳這項新服務。
The advertisement got thousands of views in just one day!
廣告在一天之內就獲得了數千次瀏覽！
Online advertising really gives us a huge advantage over competitors.
網路廣告確實給我們帶來了很大的競爭優勢。
If we keep this up, we'll definitely achieve our sales target this month.
如果繼續維持這個勢頭，我們這個月的業績目標一定達得到。
✨  Tip:  Read each sentence aloud 3 times — easy and natural! 🎯
