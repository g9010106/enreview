Unit 01 海外商務與旅行 Overseas Business & Travel
✦ Group 2 ✦ 5 Words
1
according to /əˈkɔːrdɪŋ tuː/ prep. 根據；依照
📖  as stated by someone or something
💬  According to the weather app, it's going to rain this weekend.
根據天氣預報，這個週末會下雨。
2
exhausted /ɪɡˈzɔːstɪd/ adj. 筋疲力盡的
📖  extremely tired after a lot of effort
💬  I was completely exhausted after the long flight.
長途飛行之後，我整個人累到不行。
3
arrive /əˈraɪv/ v. 到達；抵達
📖  to reach a place after travelling
💬  What time does your train arrive at the station?
你的火車幾點到站？
4
schedule /ˈʃedjuːl/ n./v. 時間表；安排
📖  a plan showing when things will happen
💬  Let me check my schedule before I confirm the meeting.
讓我先看一下行程表，再確認開會時間。
5
arrangement /əˈreɪndʒmənt/ n. 安排；佈置
📖  a plan or preparation made in advance
💬  She made all the travel arrangements before the trip.
她在出發前就把所有旅遊安排都弄好了。
💬 Dialogue  情境對話
According to the app, our train arrives at 9 PM, but I'm already exhausted from waiting.
根據APP顯示，我們的火車晚上九點到，但我等到現在已經累壞了。
I checked the schedule earlier and there might be a delay.
我之前查過時刻表，好像會誤點。
Good thing we made our accommodation arrangements in advance, so there's no rush.
還好我們之前就把住宿都安排好了，所以也不用趕。
Let's just relax — we planned everything well!
放輕鬆就好——我們什麼都計畫好了！
✨  Tip:  Read each sentence aloud 3 times — easy and natural! 🎯
