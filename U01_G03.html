Unit 01 海外商務與旅行 Overseas Business & Travel
✦ Group 3 ✦ 5 Words
🔊 速度：
1
experience /ɪkˈspɪəriəns/ n./v. 經驗；體驗
📖  something you live through that teaches you something
💬  Working abroad was the most valuable experience of my life.
在海外工作是我人生中最寶貴的經歷。
2
communicate /kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ v. 溝通；交流
📖  to share information or talk with others
💬  It's important to communicate clearly when working with overseas clients.
跟海外客戶合作時，清楚地溝通很重要。
3
communication /kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/ n. 溝通；通訊
📖  the act of sharing information or ideas
💬  Good communication helps avoid misunderstandings.
良好的溝通能避免誤解。
4
cultural /ˈkʌltʃərəl/ adj. 文化的；文化上的
📖  relating to the customs and traditions of a place
💬  I really enjoy learning about cultural differences when I travel.
我很喜歡旅行時了解不同的文化差異。
5
international /ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl/ adj. 國際的；世界的
📖  involving more than one country
💬  She works for an international company with offices in ten countries.
她在一家有十個國家辦公室的國際公司工作。
💬 Dialogue  情境對話
Working abroad has been such an amazing experience.
在海外工作真的是很棒的經歷。
I've learned to communicate better in English and understand cultural differences I never expected.
我學會了用更好的方式用英文溝通，也了解了很多意想不到的文化差異。
Good communication is key in an international environment.
在國際環境裡，良好的溝通是關鍵。
I'm so glad I took this opportunity!
我很慶幸自己把握了這個機會！
✨  Tip:  Read each sentence aloud 3 times — easy and natural! 🎯
