1 abroad /əˈbrɔːd/ adv. 在海外；出國
1. She travels abroad for business every year. 她每年出國出差。
2. Our machines sell very well abroad. 我們的機器在海外銷售非常好。
3. Going abroad helps you meet new clients. 出國幫助你認識新客戶。
2 airline /ˈeərlaɪn/ n. 航空公司
1. Which airline do you prefer for long flights? 長途飛行你偏好哪家航空公司？
2. She booked tickets through the airline website. 她透過航空公司網站訂了機票。
3. The airline lost our luggage once. 航空公司有一次弄丟了我們的行李。
3 accommodation /əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃən/ n. 住宿；住所
1. Please arrange accommodation near the factory. 請安排工廠附近的住宿。
2. The accommodation was clean and comfortable. 住宿乾淨又舒適。
3. We booked accommodation for five nights. 我們訂了五個晚上的住宿。
4 opportunity /ˌɒpəˈtjuːnɪti/ n. 機會；良機
1. This trade show is a great opportunity. 這個貿易展是個很好的機會。
2. She never misses an opportunity to learn. 她從不錯過學習的機會。
3. We found an opportunity to expand abroad. 我們找到了海外擴展的機會。
5 adventure /ədˈventʃər/ n. 冒險；冒險經歷
1. Every business trip is an adventure. 每次出差都是一次冒險。
2. She loves the adventure of meeting new clients. 她喜歡認識新客戶的冒險。
3. Starting a new business is a big adventure. 創業是一次大冒險。
