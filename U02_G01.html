Unit 02 健康、身體與醫療 Health, Body & Medical
✦ Group 1 ✦ 5 Words
🔊 速度：
1
accident /ˈæksɪdənt/ n. 意外；事故
📖  an unexpected event that causes injury or damage
💬  I slipped on the wet floor — luckily it was just a small accident.
我在濕滑的地板上滑倒了——還好只是小意外。
2
ankle /ˈæŋkəl/ n. 腳踝
📖  the joint connecting your foot and leg
💬  I twisted my ankle while jogging in the park this morning.
今天早上慢跑的時候，我扭傷了腳踝。
3
dentist /ˈdentɪst/ n. 牙醫；牙科醫生
📖  a doctor who takes care of your teeth
💬  I need to book an appointment with the dentist this week.
我這週需要預約看牙醫。
4
ill /ɪl/ adj. 生病的；不適的
📖  not feeling well; sick
💬  She felt ill after eating street food last night.
她昨晚吃了路邊攤之後感覺不舒服。
5
illness /ˈɪlnəs/ n. 疾病；生病
📖  a disease or period of being sick
💬  His illness kept him in bed for almost a whole week.
他的病讓他臥床將近一整個禮拜。
💬 Dialogue  情境對話
I had a bit of an accident yesterday — I fell and hurt my ankle.
我昨天發生了一點小意外——我跌倒然後傷到腳踝。
It's quite swollen, so I might see the dentist too to check if I knocked my tooth.
腫得蠻嚴重的，我可能也要順便去看個牙醫，因為牙齒好像也撞到了。
I've been feeling ill all day.
我整天都覺得不舒服。
These small injuries and illnesses always happen at the worst time!
這種小傷小病偏偏就在最不湊巧的時候發生！
✨  Tip:  Read each sentence aloud 3 times — easy and natural! 🎯
